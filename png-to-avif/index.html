How to Convert PNG to AVIF Online Securely

Converting a JPEG (JPG) image to a PNG is one of the most common web operations, usually required when you need to preserve intricate details without lossy artifacts, or when you are preparing assets that may eventually require transparent backgrounds.

Why convert PNG to AVIF?

JPG relies on lossy compression. Every time you save a JPG, it loses a fractional amount of quality. PNG, on the other hand, is a lossless format. While it doesn't miraculously restore lost data from your JPG, saving an image as an AVIF prevents any further deterioration. It is also the web standard for images that require transparency, making it a critical format for logos, web UI components, and digital illustrations.
