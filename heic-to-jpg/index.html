How to Convert HEIC to JPG Online Securely

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default image format for modern iPhones and iPads. While it offers excellent compression and quality, it is not always compatible with Windows, Android, or certain web platforms. Converting HEIC to JPG is the most reliable way to ensure your photos can be viewed and edited by anyone, anywhere.

Why convert HEIC to JPG?

JPG is the most universally supported image format in the world. By converting your HEIC files to JPG, you eliminate "file not supported" errors when uploading to websites, sending via email, or opening in older photo editing software. Our converter preserves the metadata and visual quality of your original iPhone photos while ensuring they are ready for any device.

Is this tool safe to use?

Yes. This tool runs 100% inside your browser. We do not upload your personal photos to any server. Your device handles the decoding and conversion locally, guaranteeing that your private memories stay private. This is the fastest and most secure way to handle batch HEIC conversion without installing bulky software.
