Before You Contact Us

Since Image Converter is 100% client-side, we never have access to your files. If a conversion fails, the issue is always on the browser side. Here are the most common fixes:

  • HEIC not converting? Make sure the heic2any library has fully loaded (wait 2–3 seconds after page load on slow connections).
  • Image looks wrong? Try a hard refresh — Ctrl + Shift + R — to clear any cached version of the app.
  • Download not starting? Check that your browser isn't blocking automatic downloads from this domain.
