About Image Converter

Image Converter was built out of frustration with online tools that forced us to upload massive images onto their servers—only to wait minutes for it to download in lower quality.

We utilize the raw native power of HTML5 `canvas.toBlob()` to perform identical lossy and lossless algorithms entirely via your local graphics processor. This means infinite scale, zero loading bars natively, and absolute privacy.
