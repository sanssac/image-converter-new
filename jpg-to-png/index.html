How to Convert JPG to PNG Online Securely

Converting a JPEG (JPG) image to a PNG is one of the most common web operations, usually required when you need to preserve intricate details without lossy artifacts, or when you are preparing assets that may eventually require transparent backgrounds.

Why convert JPG to PNG?

JPG relies on lossy compression. Every time you save a JPG, it loses a fractional amount of quality. PNG, on the other hand, is a lossless format. While it doesn't miraculously restore lost data from your JPG, saving an image as a PNG prevents any further deterioration.
